# SOP: Staff Training Session

## Purpose
Train restaurant staff to use Square POS, handle online orders, and manage QR table ordering.

## Format
- Duration: 60 minutes
- Attendees: owner/manager + 1–2 key staff
- Location: on-site (preferred) or remote via Zoom/Google Meet

---

## Training Agenda

### 1. Square POS Basics (15 min)
- Logging in and switching between staff profiles
- Taking a dine-in order
- Applying modifiers (size, add-ons, special requests)
- Processing payment — card, cash, split bill
- Voiding an item vs. refunding an order
- Printing or reprinting a receipt
- Opening and closing the cash drawer

### 2. Online Order Management (15 min)
- Where online orders appear on the POS
- Accepting / preparing / completing an order
- Adjusting prep time if kitchen is busy
- Handling a customer cancellation or refund
- Checking order history and customer details
- Temporarily pausing online ordering (busy periods)

### 3. QR Table Ordering (10 min)
- How customers see and use the QR menu
- How QR orders appear on the POS (tagged by table)
- Handling a QR order that needs modification
- What to do if a customer has trouble scanning
- Reprinting a QR code if damaged

### 4. Square Dashboard Overview (10 min)
- Accessing the dashboard (browser-based)
- Viewing daily sales summary
- Editing a menu item (price change, hide/unhide)
- Checking online ordering settings
- Where to find reports (sales by item, by hour, by day)

### 5. Troubleshooting (10 min)
- POS not receiving orders → check internet, restart app
- Printer not printing → check connection, paper, power cycle
- QR code not scanning → check URL, regenerate code
- Customer complaints about online order → how to refund in Square
- Who to contact for support (Newcastle Hub, Square AU)

---

## Training Handover Document

After training, provide the client with:

1. **Quick Reference Card** (1-page PDF)
   - How to take an order
   - How to accept an online order
   - How to refund
   - How to pause online ordering
   - Support contact numbers

2. **Login Details Summary**
   - Square Dashboard URL
   - Staff PIN codes (if configured)
   - Wi-Fi network details (for hardware)

3. **QR Code Assets**
   - Print-ready files
   - Spare codes for replacement

---

- [ ] Send training summary email within 24 hours

- [ ] Offer 1 week of priority support for questions
