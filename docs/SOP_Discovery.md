# SOP: Discovery Call & Data Collection

## Purpose
Gather all information needed to configure Square POS, Online Ordering, and QR Table Ordering for a Newcastle restaurant client.

## Duration
30–45 minutes (call) + 1–2 days (client provides assets)

## Discovery Call Script

### Opening (2 min)
- Confirm restaurant name, location, and contact person
- Explain the process: "We'll go through your menu, operations, and goals. After this call, I'll send you a checklist of what we need."

### Current Setup (5 min)
- What POS are you currently using? (or none?)
- Do you currently offer online ordering? Through what platform?
- Are you on UberEats / DoorDash / Menulog? What percentage of orders come through them?
- Do you have a website? Domain name?

### Menu & Operations (15 min)
- How many menu items (roughly)?
- Do you have modifiers? (e.g., size, add-ons, dietary options)
- How many categories? (mains, sides, drinks, desserts, specials)
- Do you run different menus? (lunch vs dinner, weekday vs weekend)
- Trading hours — dine-in, pickup, delivery (if applicable)
- Do you want to offer delivery? Own drivers or third-party?
- Table count and layout (for QR ordering)

### Hardware (5 min)
- Do you have an iPad? What model?
- Receipt printer? Kitchen printer?
- Cash drawer needed?
- Internet connection — Wi-Fi or wired?

### Goals & Expectations (5 min)
- What's the main goal? (reduce fees, speed up service, go digital)
- Any specific pain points with current setup?
- Timeline — when do you want to go live?

### Wrap-up (3 min)
- Explain next steps: "I'll send you a checklist today. Once you send back the menu and assets, we'll start the build."
- Confirm preferred communication channel (email, phone, WhatsApp)

## Client Checklist (send after call)

| Item | Required | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| Full menu (items, modifiers, prices) | Yes | Spreadsheet, PDF, or photos |
| Logo (PNG/SVG, high-res) | Yes | For Square Online and QR assets |
| Food photos | Recommended | At least hero items |
| Trading hours | Yes | Dine-in, pickup, delivery separately |
| Delivery zones and fees | If applicable | Suburb list or radius |
| Phone number | Yes | For Square profile |
| Email address | Yes | For Square account |
| Street address | Yes | For Google/Square |
| Bank account details | Yes | For Square payouts |
| Domain name + DNS access | If connecting | Registrar login or nameservers |
| Printer model(s) | If existing | Star, Epson, etc. |
| Table count + layout sketch | For QR ordering | Rough is fine |

## Red Flags (consider declining or adjusting scope)

- Client wants to use a POS other than Square → out of scope
- Client expects custom app development → out of scope
- Menu has 500+ items with complex modifiers → may need extended timeline
- Client has no budget clarity → confirm $3,500+ before proceeding
- Client wants to manage UberEats integration → strategy guidance only
- Multiple locations → quote each separately
