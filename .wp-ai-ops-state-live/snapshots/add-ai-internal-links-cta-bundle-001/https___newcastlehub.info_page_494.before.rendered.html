Quote

Design

We'll create and showcase a preliminary homepage design for your consideration and endorsement. At this stage, we eagerly await your feedback, and we'll promptly implement any required adjustments until you are completely satisfied with the design. This final homepage design will serve as the foundation for the entire website, underscoring the significance of achieving precision and excellence in this phase.

Content

During this phase, our focus shifts to the meticulous arrangement of the content you've supplied for your website in a visually appealing and coherent manner. This content encompasses a wide array of elements such as text, images, videos, call-to-action banners, graphics, accreditation and award badges, and more. If, at any point in the project, you find yourself in need of assistance in content creation, rest assured that New castlehub can provide a separate quote for this service. We have a proficient in-house team consisting of writers, photographers, videographers, and graphic designers at your disposal.

Review

The entire website construction process is now complete, with all functionalities seamlessly integrated and the content meticulously placed. It's time for the final inspection to ensure that everything aligns with your expectations. It's worth noting that websites, much like the home you reside in, are never truly 'finished.' You have the flexibility to continuously modify, update, and enhance your website as needed. If you come across any changes that you'd like to implement, please provide us with your feedback so that we can prepare for the Quality Assurance (QA) phase and make your desired adjustments.

[/ux_text] [/featured_box] [/col] [col span="6" span__sm="12"] [featured_box img="520" pos="center"] [ux_text text_color="rgb(255, 255, 255)"]

Live and Quality Assurance

Undoubtedly one of the most crucial phases, our team of technical experts conducts a comprehensive assessment of the website to uphold the highest quality standards. This entails a meticulous review covering various aspects such as spellings, broken links, image file sizes, meta tags, functionality of enquiry forms, and website loading speed. Any issues identified during this process are promptly addressed and rectified.

Once your new website successfully clears the Quality Assurance phase, it's time for the exciting moment: your website goes live and is accessible online! Following the QA approval, we activate the website, allowing you to start reaping the benefits it brings to you or your business.
